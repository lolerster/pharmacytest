Why make compounded medications?

A compounded medication can be an excellent option to patients who:

  • Have difficulty swallowing commercially available tablets and capsules.
  • Are allergic or sensitive to preservatives and dyes.
  • Need medication strengths or concentrations not commercially available.
  • React to a medicine’s taste.

Types of specialty compounding available:

Creams and Ointments
Solutions, Emulsions & Suspensions
Capsules
Suppositories
